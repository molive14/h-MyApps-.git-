What is the Chase Interview App?

The Chase Interview App is a dynamic app that allows for chase employees to log and capture data on upcoming interviews with potential job candidates for hire. The app features a homepage, interview page, candidate page, faq page, login screen and the about page you are currently viewing. If you are a chase employee and you happen to login all your data or the interview forms that you generate will be saved to your account id. Once logged in you will be able to generate interviews from the interview page and see the data projected on the candidates page. You will also be able to modify the data captured through an html form on the candidates page.
